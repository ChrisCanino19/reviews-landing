FAQ

Is this compliant?

Yes: no incentives, no gating, only to consented contacts. Every email includes a clear opt‑out.

What do you need from us?

Your Google review link or QR, a consented CSV of contacts, brand logo/colors, and a sender domain (or we can use a sending service).

Do you send the emails?

We can schedule and send via your provider or hand off assets for your team—your choice.

How fast is setup?

48 hours (business). Pro includes a 24h rush option. If we miss the 48h pre‑send window, you can request a refund.

Do you handle Spanish?

Yes. All packages support English and Spanish; Standard and Pro include fully bilingual templates.

How is data handled?

Data is used only to send review requests for the Client. Suppression lists are honored. Data is deleted upon request or 30 days after project completion. We never sell or share your data.
