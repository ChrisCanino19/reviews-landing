Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service compliant with Google's review policies?
Yes, we strictly follow Google's review guidelines. We never offer incentives for reviews, don't gate reviews, and always include a clear opt-out option in every email we send.
How quickly can I get started?
After purchase, you'll complete a brief onboarding process. We'll launch your review request campaign within 48 hours, or 24 hours for the Rush/Pro package.
What information do you need from me?
We need your Google review link or QR code, a consented contact list of recent customers, your preferred sender email address, and your logo/brand tone information.
How is customer data handled?
We only use the contact list you provide and never share it with third parties. All customer data is securely stored and deleted after the campaign is complete.
What email domain will be used to send requests?
We can use your business email domain or our dedicated sending domain. Both options are configured for optimal deliverability and compliance.
What kind of results can I expect?
Results vary by industry and customer satisfaction, but our clients typically see a 20-40% increase in Google reviews within the first month of using our service.
