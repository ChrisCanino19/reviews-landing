Privacy / Privacidad

Resumen (ES): Usamos sus datos únicamente para enviar solicitudes de reseñas para el Cliente. Respetamos listas de supresión. Eliminamos los datos a pedido o 30 días después de completar el proyecto. No vendemos ni compartimos datos. Sin incentivos ni “review gating”.

English

Purpose. We use contact data solely to set up and send (or prepare) review request emails for the Client.

Consent. Clients must provide only consenting contacts. Every email includes a clear opt‑out.

Data handling & retention. Suppression lists are honored. Data is deleted upon request or 30 days after project completion, whichever comes first. We do not sell or share data.

Security. We use industry‑standard safeguards for files and email systems. Do not email us sensitive data.

Access & deletion. Email us to access or delete data related to your project.

Compliance. No incentives. No review gating. Emails only to consenting contacts with clear opt‑out.
